Mission Statement

The Fall Church Force Wrestling Club is dedicated to developing the character and overall athleticism of youth athletes in the Falls Church area through the challenging sport of wrestling.

JOIN THE FORCE THIS SPRING TO LEARN

OLYMPIC-STYLE WRESTLING
The Spring Freestyle/Greco season is for the serious wrestler with 2 or more years of experience who has experience with foundational wrestling techniques and is ready to learn or improve upon the Olympic-style of Wrestling.
*Athletes must have a current USA Wrestling Card to participate

Date & Location:
April 5th through May 31st, 2022

Practices will be held in the wrestling room on the lower level of Meridian High School in Falls Church, VA
Cost: $100

Practice Days/Times:
Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6:30pm - 8:30pm

The Force Freestyle/Greco season is reserved for wrestlers with 2 or more years of experience. Please email theforcewc@gmail.com if you are interested in discussing an exception.
